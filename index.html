Momo from the Himalayas

-Presenting Nepalese style chicken dumpling recipe

Ingredients:

For Dumplings

  • One dumplings wrapper
  • One pound ground chicken
  • Two medium sized onions
  • Quarter cup cabbage
  • One cup spring onions
  • One cup cilantro
  • Half tablespoon garlic paste
  • Half tablespoon ginger paste
  • Half tablespoon coriander powder
  • Half tablespoon cumin powder
  • One tablespoon momo masala powder
  • Salt per taste
  • Quarter stick butter
  • Quarter cup warm water

For Tomato Sauce

  • Two tablespoon oil
  • Two medium sized tomatoes
  • Three to four dried red chillies
  • Two garlic cloves
  • Half teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Quarter cup cilantro
  • Salt per taste
  • One teaspoon sugar
  • One lemon

Recipe:

To make a filling, finely chop red onion, cabbage, spring onions and cilantro. Place those chopped veggies in a large bowl. Add ground chicken on the same bowl. Add ginger paste, garlic paste, salt (as per your taste), cumin powder, coriander powder and momo masala powder (this can be easily found in any Nepali/Indian Store). Add melted butter and warm water. Mixed those ingredients together. Now, after the filling is ready, start adding those filling in each wrapper (wrapper can be found in any chinese/korean store). Momos can be wrapped in different ways. We just need to make sure the filling is properly closed in the wrapper.

After all fillings are wrapped, place the momo on the top two shelves of the steamer. Add as much momos as the steamer can fit, but make sure that each momo is one inch apart so that they don't stick to one another Now, boil water in a bottom shelf of the steamer. After he water is boiled, add the remaining shelves(containing momos) to the steamer. Steam those momos for about 15 mins.

While the momo is steaming, you can start making the sauce. In a pan, add two tablespoon oil. After the oil is heated, add cumin seeds and fry them until its golden brown in color. Add dry chillies (you can add chillies according your preference). After the chillies are slightly roasted, add chopped tomatoes and salt per taste. Cook the tomatoes until they are soft. After the tomatoes are cooked, add sugar to balance the taste. Then, remove the pan from heat. Now, add freshly chopped cilantro and one small sized lemon. Blend everything in a blender until they are smooth. The sauce is ready as well as the Momo. Serve the momo with the sauce. Enjoy!! Click here to learn more about Nepalese Foods
